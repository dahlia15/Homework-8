About Me

Welcome to my page!

My name is Dahlia Amade, and I am a beauty marketer by day and freelance musician by night. After about 5 years within the beauty industry, I have decided to begin exploring other opportunities.

Before leaving L'Oreal in 2019, I worked on an AI Hair Color tool to enhance the consumer's "research" phase within the path to purchase and encourage her to purchase with confidence. Although I did not work on the back end integration of the 3D technology into our tool, I was able to gain a lot of visibility on how front end and back end work together to create "x". I'm excited to see all that I can absorb over the next couple of months and look forward to exercising my new skills in the real world.
